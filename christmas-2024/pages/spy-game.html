Find the Spy

  • Each round is 15 minutes (10 mins of questionning + 5 mins for Investigators to discuss).
Round 1 Round 2 Round 3 Round 4 Round 5 Round 6
Suspects to Investigate 3 4 4 5 5 5

13 Agents

GOAL: Help Investigators catch the Spies. Identify ALL 5 Spies by round 6 to win.

  • All Agents get the same CORRECT code word.
  • You CANNOT say your secret code word.
  • During each round, help Investigators find the spies.
  • Investigators can ony ask Yes/No questions but Agents can say anything.

5 Spies

GOAL: Find your fellow Spies & stay hidden. You win if a Spy correctly guesses the identities of ALL Spies OR Investigators fail.

  • All Spies get the same INCORRECT code word that is similar to the correct code word.
  • You CANNOT say your secret code word.
  • Each Spy has TWO chances to text the GM (647-648-8391) their guess of who are all the Spies.
  • Spies can use their chances at ANY time before the game is over.
  • The GM will privately reveal to the Spy how many guesses were correct.
  • Spies now have the ability Sabotage! If the Spies have correctly guessed at least ONE of their fellow Spies, they get to hide the results of one person from the next investigation.

6 Investigators

GOAL: Look for the hidden Spies among Agents. You win if you identify ALL 5 Spies by round 6.

  • Investigators can only ask Yes/No questions.
  • At the end of every round, 1 Investigator will make a list of suspects & Investigate them.
  • The GM will publicly reveal how many among the list of suspects are Spies.
  • Each Investigator only gets ONE chance to Investigate their list (a different Investigator each round).
