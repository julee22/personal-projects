Find the Spy

  • There are 6 rounds.
  • Each round is 15 minutes (10 mins of questionning + 5 mins for Investigators to discuss).
  • In round 1 & 2, investigators can check 3 suspects.
  • In round 3 & 4, investigators can check 4 suspects.
  • In round 5 & 6, investigators can check 5 suspects.

10 Agents

Goal: Help the Investigator catch the spies. You win if you identify ALL 5 spies by round 5.

  • Agents received the same CORRECT code word.
  • You CANNOT say your secret code word.
  • During each round, help the Investigator by finding out who doesn't have the same code word.
  • Investigators can ony ask Yes/No questions but Agents can say anything.

5 Spies

Goal: Find your fellow spies and stay hidden till the end. You win if a spy correctly guesses all their fellow spies OR the Investigators fail to catch all of you.

  • Spies received the same INCORRECT code word.
  • You CANNOT say your secret code word.
  • Each spy has TWO chances to text the GM (647-648-8391) their guess as to who their 5 fellow spies are.
  • Spies can use their two chances at ANY time before the game is over.
  • The GM will privately reveal how many among the guesses are correct.

6 Investigators

Goal: Look for the hidden spies among the agents. You win if you identify all 5 spies by round 5.

  • Investigators can only ask Yes/No questions.
  • At the end of every round, 1 investigator will investigate a suspect list of who the spies are.
  • The GM will publicly reveal how many among the suspect list are spies.
  • Each investigator only gets ONE chance to investigate a suspect list (a different investigator each round).
