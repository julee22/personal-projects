Find the Spy

  • There are 6 rounds.
  • Each round is 15 minutes (10 mins of questionning + 5 mins for Investigators to discuss).
  • In round 1 & 2, Investigators can check 3 suspects.
  • In round 3 & 4, Investigators can check 4 suspects.
  • In round 5 & 6, Investigators can check 5 suspects.

10 Agents

GOAL: Help Investigators catch the Spies. You win if Investigators identify ALL 5 Spies by round 6.

  • All Agents get the same CORRECT code word.
  • You CANNOT say your secret code word.
  • During each round, help the Investigator by finding out who doesn't have the same code word.
  • Investigators can ony ask Yes/No questions but Agents can say anything.

5 Spies

GOAL: Find your fellow Spies and stay hidden. You win if a Spy correctly guesses the identities of ALL Spies OR Investigators fail.

  • All Spies get the same INCORRECT code word that is similar to the correct code word.
  • You CANNOT say your secret code word.
  • Each Spy has TWO chances to text the GM (647-648-8391) their guess of who are all the Spies.
  • Spies can use their TWO chances at ANY time before the game is over.
  • The GM will privately reveal to the Spy how many among the guesses are correct.

6 Investigators

GOAL: Look for the hidden Spies among Agents. You win if you identify ALL 5 Spies by round 6.

  • Investigators can only ask Yes/No questions.
  • At the end of every round, 1 Investigator will make a list of suspects and investigate them.
  • The GM will publicly reveal how many among the list of suspects are Spies.
  • Each Investigator only gets ONE chance to investigate their list (a different Investigator each round).
